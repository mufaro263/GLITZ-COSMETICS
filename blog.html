Manicure and Pedicure tips & Tricks

By Admin|in Hair Care|2 Comments

While weak, brittle nails can be attributed to heredity and normal aging, there are a few external factors that also play a part. Dietary deficiencies like a lack of iron, wearing polish longer than two weeks, not taking a break between manicures, and routinely swabbing on harsh solvents like acetone can all precipitate not-so-tough nails. To foster strength and flexibility, stay polish-free for at least one week per month and brush on nourishing jojoba oil or a topical retinoid like Tazarotene cream 0.1% to boost bendability.
