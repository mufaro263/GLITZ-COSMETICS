BRAND STATEMENT We believe in going big, being bold and feeling sexy - and that unapologetic attitude comes through in everything we do. Because here, sensuality is a strength. It's how we move through the world. Our larger-than-life, sensorial beauty stimulates your senses and amplifies what you love most about yourself, so you can feel even sexier in your skin. We live to fuel you with the confidence to go all in - and be 100% yourself, 100% of the time.

We are devoted to bringing back the ritual of applying makeup, so you feel beautiful at every step of the process. Each product tells a story, inspired by the history of real women we admire from the past.

HISTORICAL AUTHENTICITY We are dedicated to researching historical cosmetics to bring back authentic recreations of shades, packaging, and more. Frequently used in Hollywood because of our faithful reproductions, we strive to bring back the romance and glamour to the makeup industry.

OUR VEGAN AND CRUELTY-FREE PLEDGE We love our animals and strongly believe that using animal-derived raw materials in our cosmetics is completely unnecessary. We do not test any of our products on animals and we also do not use raw materials that have been tested on animals - Aromi is cruelty-free! We are certified by both PETA and Leaping Bunny. We also do not sell or ship our products in countries that require animal testing.
